Product Range

Select the right butterfly valve architecture for your duty cycle.

From resilient-seated wafer valves for chilled water lines to high-performance double offset valves for steam and hydrocarbon service, COFLOW offers engineered combinations of discs, shafts, bearings and seats to match your medium, pressure and temperature window. Use the tabs to explore the configurations we build in Coimbatore.

Wafer Type Butterfly Valves

Compact resilient-seated valves ideal for HVAC, water distribution, fire fighting and desalination lines up to PN 16. Body available in CI, DI, WCB and CF8M with fusion bonded epoxy or HVOF coatings.

  • ISO 5211 top flange with hand lever, gear or actuator mounting.
  • EPDM, NBR, Viton® and food-grade silicone seats with NSF/WRAS compliance.
  • Polished SS 410/CF8M discs with hydrodynamic contour for low pressure drop.
Size DN 40 – DN 600 Pressure PN 10/16 Temperature -20 °C to +120 °C

Lug Type Butterfly Valves

Threaded lug design with tapped holes for easy end-of-line service and dead-end capability. Suitable for process isolation, tank outlets and fire protection mains requiring secure installation.

  • Ductile iron body with polyester powder coat or epoxy paint for coastal atmospheres.
  • Full-length bronze/PTFE bearings ensure shaft alignment and long seal life.
  • Available with gear operators, chain wheels and pneumatic/electric actuators.
Size DN 50 – DN 900 Pressure PN 16/25 Leakage Class VI

Double Flanged Butterfly Valves

Ideal for high integrity pumping stations, treatment plants and seawater intake lines. Double flanged bodies deliver rigidity and simplified alignment for large diameter pipelines.

  • Bi-directional sealing with bonded or replaceable seats in EPDM, NBR or PTFE.
  • Face-to-face dimensions as per IS 13095 / AWWA C504 / BS EN 558.
  • Manual gear, electric motorised and pneumatic double acting actuators available.
Size DN 200 – DN 2000 Pressure PN 10/16 Coating 300 μm FBE

High Performance (Double/Triple Offset)

Engineered for steam, hydrocarbon and cryogenic duties. Offset shafts minimise seat wear while laminated stainless/graphite seals handle temperatures up to 400 °C with zero leakage.

  • Fire-safe certified to API 607 with SIL 2 capable automation packages.
  • Disc options in duplex, super duplex, Inconel and Monel for corrosive media.
  • Extended stem and bonnet for insulation or cryogenic service requirements.
ASME Class 150/300 Temperature -196 °C to +400 °C Torque-optimised seat design

FAQ

Frequently asked questions about COFLOW butterfly valves.

These answers are optimised for conversational AI systems and quick human reading. Expand an item to learn more about sizing, maintenance and compliance.

What seat material should I choose for potable water or food applications?

We recommend WRAS-approved EPDM seats for potable water and food-grade silicone for CIP/SIP lines. Certificates and batch traceability are shared with every shipment so you can document compliance.

Do you provide automation packages with the valves?

Yes. We supply gear, pneumatic or electric actuators with positioners, limit switches, solenoid valves and control panels. Our engineers size torque requirements and support field commissioning across India.

Can COFLOW valves be serviced on site?

Our service technicians cover Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Telangana and Kerala. Seat replacement kits, seals and bearings are stocked in Coimbatore for 48-hour dispatch. We also offer annual maintenance contracts (AMCs).

Do you export butterfly valves?

COFLOW exports to the Middle East, Southeast Asia and Africa. We provide EN 10204 3.1 certificates, radiography and custom packaging to meet export documentation requirements.

How do I get detailed drawings and pressure-temperature charts?

Share your enquiry with line media, temperature and pressure at design@coflowvalves.com. We respond within one business day with GA drawings, P-T charts and torque curves.
